HISTORY ABOUT RONALDO

Fun facts about Ronadlo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time. Here are some key points about his life and career. Youth Career: Ronaldo began playing football at a young age and joined the youth team of Andorinha, where his father worked as a kit man. He later moved to Nacional and then Sporting Lisbon, where he honed his skills.

  • Sporting Lisbon: Ronaldo made his professional debut for Sporting Lisbon in 2002, showcasing his incredible talent
  • Manchester United: In 2003, he signed with Manchester United, where he won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League in 2008. He also won his first Ballon d'Or in 2008
  • Real Madrid: Ronaldo transferred to Real Madrid in 2009 for a then-record fee. During his time there, he became the club's all-time top scorer, winning four Champions League titles and two La Liga titles, among numerous other honors
  • Juventus: In 2018, he moved to Juventus, where he continued to excel, winning two Serie A titles and becoming the league's top scorer
  • Return to Manchester United: Ronaldo returned to Manchester United in 2021, further solidifying his legacy in the Premier League
  • Al Nassr: In late 2022, he joined Saudi Arabian club Al Nassr, marking a new chapter in his career

International Career

Portugal National Team: Ronaldo has been a key player for Portugal since 2003, leading the team to victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship and the 2019 UEFA Nations League.
